Chapter 1: Electric Charges and Fields

NCERT 12th — we’ll build intuition with a live activity and short explanations.

Rub, then drag rods. Unlike ⇒ together (gap); Like ⇒ apart.
Teacher’s explanation overlay. You can talk while students try the activity.

What’s happening here?

  • Rubbing transfers charge (triboelectric effect): Glass tends to become positive; Plastic tends to become negative.
  • Like charges repel, unlike charges attract.
  • We keep a tiny “air-gap” so rods never overlap (contact modeling).
